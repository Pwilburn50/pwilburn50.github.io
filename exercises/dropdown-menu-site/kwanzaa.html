Hi, I'm Kwanzaa

"It's a happy talent to know how to play." ~ Ralph Waldo Emerson A perfect day for Kwanzaa includes playing fetch! When she's not playing fetch, Kwanzaa enjoys walking around with a ball in her mouth - squeaking it. Overall, Kwanzaa is a lower energy girl. A couple of 15-minute fetch sessions and a slow, short walk in the evening are enough exercise for her on a typical day. Kwanzaa is house trained and crate trained and is ok being crated for 8-9 hours while her foster mom is at work.
